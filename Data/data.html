City of Glendale, Arizona

Data Sharing

The City of Glendale, Arizona currently distributes GIS data as a service to the public. The City provides this service to promote the sharing of data among all governmental agencies and private GIS users in an effort to reduce overall cost of data collection. In return, the City of Glendale requests that all users of the City’s data provide non-propriety data which are created, collected or modified to the City for internal use as well as distribution to our GIS user community.
